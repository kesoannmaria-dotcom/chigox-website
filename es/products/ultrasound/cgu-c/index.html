Resumen

Descripción del producto

CGU-C es un ecógrafo inalámbrico portátil convexo diseñado para aplicaciones abdominales, obstétricas, ginecológicas y de imagen general. The device connects directly to smartphones, tablets, and computers via WiFi, providing portátil ultrasound imaging for bedside diagnosis, emergency medicine, point-of-care ultrasound (POCUS), and mobile healthcare services.

Características principales

  • Convex Array Imaging: Optimized for deep tissue visualization and abdominal examinations.
  • Wireless Connectivity: Direct WiFi connection to smartphones, tablets, and compatible devices.
  • Portable Pocket Design: Compact handheld design weighing only 230g for easy transportation and daily use.
  • Long Battery Life: Built-in 4200mAh lithium battery supports up to 3 hours of continuous operation.
  • Real-Time Imaging: Delivers clear, stable, and responsive ultrasound images.
  • Compatibilidad multiplataforma: compatible con Android, iOS y Windows.
  • Almacenamiento de imágenes y video: Allows convenient storage and review of ultrasound images and videos on connected devices.
  • Point-of-Care Ultrasonido: Suitable for bedside examinations, emergency assessments, and field applications.
