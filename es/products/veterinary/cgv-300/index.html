Resumen

Descripción del producto

CGV-300 es una solución completa de endoscopia veterinaria para procedimientos gastrointestinales, respiratorios, ORL, urinarios y extracción de cuerpos extraños. The system supports flexible endoscopes, visual foreign body forceps and rigid endoscopes, providing a versatile platform for modern veterinary clinics and hospitals.

Características principales

  • One Platform, Multiple Aplicaciones
  • Supports gastroscopy, bronchoscopy, rhinoscopy, otoscopy, urethroscopy and foreign body retrieval.
  • Multiple Display Options
  • Available with:
  • 3.5-inch Portable Display
  • 12.1-inch HD Touchscreen Workstation
  • Flexible & Endoscopio rígido Compatibility
  • Supports flexible veterinary endoscopes and rigid endoscope systems.
  • Image & Video Recording
  • Integrated image management and case documentation.
