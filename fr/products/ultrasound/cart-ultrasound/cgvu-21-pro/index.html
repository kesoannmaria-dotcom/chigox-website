Resumen

Descripción del producto

CGVU-21 Pro is a high-performance Color Doppler Échographie System featuring a 21.5-inch LCD display and a 13.3-inch touch screen, designed for clinical imaging workflows. The system offers multiple probe ports, advanced imaging technologies including Tissue Doppler Imaging (TDI), free-hand elastography, and Anatomic M Mode for precise cardiac and abdominal evaluations.

Características principales

  • 21.5-inch medical HD monitor + 13.3-inch adjustable touch screen
  • Ergonomic console for easy operation
  • Stereo audio system
  • 4 activated probe ports for multiple applications
  • Built-in graphic management system
  • Front and rear handles for mobility
  • 4 moveable wheels with locks
  • Supports multiple languages: EN, RU, SP, PT, ARB, VN, ID, DE, FR

Aplicaciones

  • Cardiac and Imagen abdominal
  • General Échographie
  • Tissue Doppler Evaluation
  • Elastography Assessments
  • Point-of-Care Échographie
