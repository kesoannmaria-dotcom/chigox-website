Panoramica

Panoramica prodotto

CGU-4P is a wireless handheld phased array ultrasound scanner designed for cardiac imaging, emergency medicine, critical care, anesthesia, and point-of-care ultrasound applications. The compact device connects directly to smartphones, tablets, and computers through WiFi, delivering portable color Doppler imaging for bedside diagnosis and rapid clinical assessment.

Caratteristiche principali

  • Phased array Cardiac Imaging: Optimized for cardiac and thoracic ultrasound examinations.
  • Color Doppler Technology: Supports Color Doppler, PW Doppler and PDI imaging.
  • Wireless Connectivity: Direct WiFi connection to smartphones, tablets and computers.
  • Puncture Guidance Function: Supports in-plane and out-of-plane needle guidance.
  • Automatic Vessel Misura: Built-in vascular measurement functions.
  • Portable Pocket Design: Compact design weighing only 140g.
  • Multi-Platform Compatibility: Supports Android, iOS and Windows systems.
  • Point-of-Care Ecografia Solution: Ideal for bedside examinations, emergency medicine and critical care.
