제품 개요

CGV-G is a veterinary gastroscope series designed for upper gastrointestinal examinations, biopsy procedures and foreign body retrieval. Compatible with the CGV-300 동물병원 Endoscopy Solution. Available Models 모델 외경 작업 채널 작업 길이 CGV-G95 9.5mm 3.0mm 1050mm CGV-G80 8.0mm 2.6mm 1500mm CGV-G70 7.0mm 2.0mm 3000mm

주요 특징

  • HD Image Quality
  • Clear visualization of the digestive tract.
  • Multiple Diameter Options
  • Suitable for different animal sizes.
  • Therapeutic Capability
  • Supports biopsy and foreign body removal procedures.
  • Waterproof Design
  • Easy cleaning and disinfection.
  • 적용 분야
  • Esophageal Examination
