Opis produktu

CGE-100 is a Full HD 1080P medical endoscopy platform designed for gastrointestinal, respiratory, ENT, urology and rigid endoscopy procedures. The system consists of a video processor, LED cold light source, monitor, and optional workstation software. One processor can support multiple types of flexible and rigid endoscopes, providing a scalable solution for hospitals, clinics and endoscopy centers.

Kluczowe cechy

  • The CGE-100 system supports an optional medical workstation for efficient data management and clinical documentation.
  • Patient information management
  • Image and video capture
  • Procedure recording
  • Case database management
  • Report generation
  • Image annotation
  • USB data export
