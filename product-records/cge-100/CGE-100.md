## Product Overview

CGE-100 is a Full HD 1080P medical endoscopy platform designed for
gastrointestinal, respiratory, ENT, urology and rigid endoscopy procedures.

The system consists of a video processor, LED cold light source, monitor, and
optional workstation software. One processor can support multiple types of
flexible and rigid endoscopes, providing a scalable solution for hospitals,
clinics and endoscopy centers.

## Key Features

- Full HD 1080P imaging for clear clinical visualization.
- One processor supports multiple flexible and rigid endoscopes.
- Scalable configuration for hospitals, clinics and endoscopy centers.
- Optional medical workstation for patient data, images, videos and reports.
- Supports USB data export, network sharing, report printing and EMR integration.

## Applications

- Gastroenterology: gastroscopy, colonoscopy, biopsy and foreign body retrieval
- Pulmonology: bronchoscopy and airway examination
- ENT: nasopharyngoscopy and throat examination
- Urology: flexible cystoscopy
- Surgery: rigid endoscopy, ENT surgery, urology surgery and laparoscopy
