Overview

Product Overview

CGU-C is a wireless handheld convex ultrasound scanner designed for abdominal, obstetric, gynecological, and general imaging applications. The device connects directly to smartphones, tablets, and computers via WiFi, providing portable ultrasound imaging for bedside diagnosis, emergency medicine, point-of-care ultrasound (POCUS), and mobile healthcare services.

Key Features

  • Convex Array Imaging: Optimized for deep tissue visualization and abdominal examinations.
  • Wireless Connectivity: Direct WiFi connection to smartphones, tablets, and compatible devices.
  • Portable Pocket Design: Compact handheld design weighing only 230g for easy transportation and daily use.
  • Long Battery Life: Built-in 4200mAh lithium battery supports up to 3 hours of continuous operation.
  • Real-Time Imaging: Delivers clear, stable, and responsive ultrasound images.
  • Multi-Platform Compatibility: Compatible with Android, iOS, and Windows operating systems.
  • Image & Video Storage: Allows convenient storage and review of ultrasound images and videos on connected devices.
  • Point-of-Care Ultrasound: Suitable for bedside examinations, emergency assessments, and field applications.
