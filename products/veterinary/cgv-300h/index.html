Overview

Product Overview

CGV-300H is a portable high-definition veterinary endoscopy platform integrating a video processor, LED cold light source, air/water supply system and 12-inch monitor. The system is compatible with flexible and rigid endoscopes, allowing veterinarians to perform gastrointestinal, respiratory, ENT, urology and minimally invasive examinations using a single imaging platform.

Key Features

  • Portable All-in-One Design: Integrated monitor, processor, light source and air pump.
  • Compatible With Multiple Endoscopes: One processor supports multiple endoscope types.
  • Full HD Imaging: 1920 × 1080 resolution for clear mucosal observation.
  • Built-in Recording Function: Photo capture and video recording with 64GB SD card.
  • 12-inch Foldable Display: Portable and convenient for transport.
  • Powerful LED Light Source: 80W LED cold light source.
  • Air & Water Supply System: Integrated air and water system for clinical procedures.
  • Multi-Application Platform: Suitable for gastrointestinal, respiratory, ENT and rigid endoscopy applications.

Applications

  • Biopsy Procedures
  • Minimally Invasive Veterinary Surgery
