Overview

Product Overview

CGV-DR2 is a portable mobile veterinary X-ray machine designed for outdoor and clinical animal radiography. It features compact size, light weight, built-in lithium battery and wireless exposure control, making it suitable for veterinary hospitals, horse farms, pastures, zoos and research institutions.

Key Features

  • Small and lightweight
  • Built-in lithium battery
  • 1.6kW X-ray power unit
  • Wired and wireless exposure modes
  • Wireless remote control
  • Bluetooth support
  • Easy computer connection
  • 5 preset exposure parameters
  • TOF SID measurement

Applications

  • Equine X-ray
  • Pet radiography
  • Field diagnosis
  • Mobile veterinary imaging
  • Zoo animal examination
  • Pasture and farm animal diagnosis
