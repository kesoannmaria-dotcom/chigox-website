Genel Bakış

Ürün Özeti

CGU-C; abdominal, obstetrik, jinekolojik ve genel görüntüleme uygulamaları için tasarlanmış kablosuz el tipi konveks ultrason tarayıcıdır. The device connects directly to smartphones, tablets, and computers via WiFi, providing taşınabilir ultrasound imaging for bedside diagnosis, emergency medicine, point-of-care ultrasound (POCUS), and mobile healthcare services.

Başlıca Özellikler

  • Konveks dizi Imaging: Optimized for deep tissue visualization and abdominal examinations.
  • Wireless Connectivity: Direct WiFi connection to smartphones, tablets, and compatible devices.
  • Portable Pocket Design: Compact handheld design weighing only 230g for easy transportation and daily use.
  • Long Battery Life: Built-in 4200mAh lithium battery supports up to 3 hours of continuous operation.
  • Real-Time Imaging: Delivers clear, stable, and responsive ultrasound images.
  • Çoklu platform uyumluluğu: Android, iOS ve Windows işletim sistemleriyle uyumludur.
  • Görüntü ve video depolama: Allows convenient storage and review of ultrasound images and videos on connected devices.
  • Point-of-Care Ultrason: Suitable for bedside examinations, emergency assessments, and field applications.
